About Us

The Ife Adebayo Development Foundation is a not-for-profit and non-political organisation devoted to improving the quality of humanity and the community by providing scholarships, education support grants and other free incentives to promote health, education and development of the under-served populace in our society.

  

Objectives

  • arrow_circle_right To help humanity and community development.
  • arrow_circle_right To provide aid, support and empowerment to the less privileged people in the society.
  • arrow_circle_right To support developmental projects in our society.
  • arrow_circle_right To run special programmes aimed at liberating and empowering the aged, orphans and widows
    and improving means of livelihood and social dignity
  • arrow_circle_right To promote the health, education and development of the vulnerable populace through the
    award of scholarships, education support grants and the provision of other free incentives.
  • arrow_circle_right To promote the spirit of self-help and self-reliance among the members of the Foundation.
  • arrow_circle_right To work with any individual, Group or association having common objectives with the Foundation.
     

Founder

Ife Adebayo is the founder of the Ife Adebayo Development Foundation. In 2019, the Foundation was primarily set up to promote community development; by catering for the less privileged members of our society, and by providing assistance, support, and empowerment to those living in underserved areas..

  
               

Our Vision

Through education, technology and innovation support, and empowerment, less privileged residents in underserved communities will be better equipped to enhance their means of subsistence and become problem solvers who promote development in their communities.

   
     

Our Mission

Ife Adebayo
founder of the Ife Adebayo Development Foundation

Ife Adebayo is a proven leader in the fields of strategy, policy creation and implementation in digital technology and creativity. He served as the special adviser to the office of the vice president of Nigeria from 2015 - 2023, where he provided detailed policy recommendations to the Nigerian Vice President (and the President) in the areas of digital technology, innovation, and creativity.

He obtained a Master of Business Information Systems(MBA) degree in 2009 from the University of East London and held a BE. Electrical and Electronics Engineering - Computer and Control Major, from the University of Ilorin.

He actively volunteers in humanitarian and community development projects and has actively served as:
Local Committee Vice President (Projects) of AIESEC Ilorin, Nigeria from 2003 - 2006 Volunteer with Slum2School Africa from 2013 - Present

Youth Leader of Action Congress UK – ACN UK (now APC UK after party merger) from 2010 - 2011.

Member of ActNow Initiative, Lagos, Nigeria from 2013 - Present. In 2013, He received a prestigious award from The Future AwardsAfrica in the Community Action category for charitable works in education.
